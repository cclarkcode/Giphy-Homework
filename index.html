Instructions

  1. First, choose a genre, or make your own (If you make your own, you'll start with no buttons)
  2. Use the input box to add buttons to your Giphy page
  3. Click a button to get a selection of GIFs based on your button
  4. Click on a GIF to animate it
  5. You can go back to genre selection by hitting Reset
